Why Fediverse?

The fediverse represents the next evolution of community-based social media. The fediverse is a social network made up of a collection of thousands of servers running open-source software that can communicate with each other across common protocols.

The primary advantage offered by the fediverse is that it allows any individual or group to be their own social media provider while also connecting them to the larger community of fediverse participants.

The fediverse runs on tens of thousands of servers (aka instances) and has a population of between 1 million and 5 million active users.
