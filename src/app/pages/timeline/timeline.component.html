The movement to DRS GME was born in the community reddit.com/r/superstonk
This is where legitimate investors of GameStop learned that to protect their interests they must directly register their shares of GME in their own name.
However, for reasons, the superstonk community started to encounter issues with Reddit.
This problem is solved by embracing the fediverse, so that legitimate investors of GameStop can continue the conversation around DRS GME without fear of censorship.
